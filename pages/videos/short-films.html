Year Round

Unaffiliated presents their second film, 'Year Round', capturing the crew throughout the 13-14 season as they ski and snowboard the Midwest and Mt. Hood Oregon. Featuring Paul Marik, Lee Pinkerton, Cody Larson, Zach Lastrilla, Seth Leinbach, Conner Easton, Dan Klonowski, Jon Fox, John Knopf. Filmed by Ryan Ruffing, Zach Lastrilla, Seth Leinbach. Shot at Granite Peak, Tyrol Basin, Sunburst, and Mt. Hood.
